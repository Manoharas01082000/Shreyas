MEDICAL TREATMENTS

Shreyas follows a holistic approach in treating and managing the ailments through Ayurveda, Panchakarma, Naturopathy, Detox Diet, Yoga, Pranayama, Meditation, and Counselling sessions. Based on the specific condition, chronicity, affected body part or system (nervous system, digestive system, etc.), past illness, allergic factors, and with the consolidated inputs from our in-house Ayurveda and Naturopathy consultants, Yoga and Meditation Teachers, an appropriate treatment plan is scheduled that hastens the convalescence period.

Ailments Treated And Managed:

MUSCULOSKELETAL DISORDERS

Musculoskeletal disorders constitute a major cause of disability and morbidity globally. We not only offer a wide range of therapies but also educate people to have a better understanding of healthy musculoskeletal ageing and risk factors associated with premature ageing.

  • Pain management
  • Tendonitis
  • Sciatica
  • Frozen Shoulder
  • Gout
  • Tennis Elbow
  • Calcaneal Spur
  • Spondylitis
  • Scoliosis
  • Osteoporosis/Osteopenia
  • Osteoarthritis
  • Rheumatoid Arthritis
  • Herniated/Ruptured Disc
  • Disc Bulge
  • Ligament Strain/Inflammation
  • Carpal/Radial Tunnel Syndrome
